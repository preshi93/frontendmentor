Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cokked to perfect, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.


    Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Peparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling; cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat or herbs

Instrustion

  1. Beat the eggs: In the bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tabblespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over half of the omelette.
  5. Beat the eggs: In the bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tabblespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  6. Fold and serve: As the omeletter continues to cook, carefully lift one of the edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  7. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additonal salt and pepper if needed.
